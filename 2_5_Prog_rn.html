How to Run

Beginners should work out in a way that gradually increases their endurance by running and gradually increasing their time and distance.

You may be out of breath because it's your first time, but if you endure it and overcome it, you're a beginner, so I think your physical strength will increase quickly.
Outside the beginner level, you should set your target based on the previous experiences and move on gradually.

Since you have some confidence, you should take care not to overdo it and go to injury. Also, you should upgrade yourself with a little more time and money because running is before or already your daily routine.
If you're already a superhuman, you should just do what you want. Achieve your dreams on the international stage by training with professional programs and advanced science.
