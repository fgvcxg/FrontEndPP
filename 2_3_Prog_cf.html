Rock The Bells is a 6-week kettlebell program with everything you need to get strong and lean, There are 3 workouts per week for the first two weeks. Each workout has a watm-up, a strength, and a core section designed to provide optimum result. This is followed by a week of challenges designed to demonstrate your newly-found strength. This cycle repeats with progressions.
